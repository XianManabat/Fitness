The 4 Principles

Progressive Overload

Increase reps or dumbbell weight every 1–2 weeks. Muscle grows when challenged, not comfortable.

Caloric Deficit

Eat ~200–300 kcal below maintenance (~2,100 kcal/day). Lose fat without sacrificing muscle.

High Protein

Target 120–130g protein/day (≈2g per kg). Protein preserves muscle while you cut fat.

Consistency Over Intensity

4 days/week, every week, beats 7-day bursts followed by rest. Show up even at 60%.

Belly Fat Key Rules: Cut softdrinks completely. No alcohol. Reduce white rice at dinner. Sleep 7–8 hrs (cortisol = fat storage). Drink 2–3L water daily. Tummy fat responds to consistent caloric deficit — you can't spot-reduce it with crunches alone.
Progress Checkpoints

Weeks 1–4 (Foundation)

  • Build workout habit
  • Learn proper form
  • Lose 0.5–1 kg
  • Feel less bloated

Weeks 5–8 (Progress)

  • Increase weights/reps
  • Visible ab definition starting
  • Lose 1–2 more kg
  • Endurance improves

Weeks 9–12 (Results)

  • Lean muscle visible
  • Belly significantly flatter
  • Total: 3–5 kg fat lost
  • Noticeable body recomp
